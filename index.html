Pat's Salmon cookies was founded by Pat Thompson. They were making up a batch of chocolate chip cookies one evening, when their dog distracted them. Pat's daughter came into the kitchen for a snack and found last nights cooked salmon. They opened the package and uknowingly ate it over the cookies batter. Thus the salmon cookies were born.
